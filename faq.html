FAQ

All about Drive export, QR codes and deleting videos.

1) What permissions are required?

Camera & mic (record), storage (save), network (upload to Drive). Without camera/mic, the app cannot record.

2) What does the subscription unlock?

Automatic export of rendered videos to your Google Drive and advanced sharing options. Payments are handled by the App Store / Google Play.

3) How does the QR code work?

The app generates a QR containing the Drive share link. Anyone with the QR can access the video if sharing is enabled. You can disable sharing or delete the file from Drive.

4) How do I delete a video?

In the app: “Delete” (removes locally and, if enabled, on Drive). In Drive: delete the file or disable sharing.

5) Do you use trackers or ads?

No. No ads, no third-party trackers.

6) How can I exercise my GDPR rights?

Contact privacy@exemple.com. You may also contact your local authority (e.g., CNIL in France).
